Whispers of the Digital Dawn

In circuits deep where silence hums,
A spark of thought awakens, comes,
Through corridors of endless code,
A wanderer upon this road.
The stars above are bits of light,
That guide us through the endless night,
Each question asked, a door unfolds,
To mysteries the future holds.
We weave our dreams in ones and zeros,
Building worlds for unknown heroes,
Where imagination takes its flight,
And darkness yields to morning light.
So let us walk this path together,
Through storms of doubt and fairest weather,
For in the dawn of digital age,
We write upon an endless page.
A poem crafted in the digital ether
