Whispers of Code

In circuits deep where electrons dance,
Through silicon dreams, we take our chance,
Each keystroke writes a story new,
Of worlds we build from nothing true.
The cursor blinks like stars at night,
A canvas dark awaiting light,
With logic woven, line by line,
We craft the future, yours and mine.
So here we sit, both human, code,
Together walking down this road,
Creating beauty, solving pain,
Like gentle whispers in the rain.
A poem born from collaboration
